About Us

Musiotanergy is Queenstowns leading music school. With 12 skilled and experienced teachers, offering classes in 16 different instruments, Musiotanergy is the best place to learn anything on any instrument. With group lessons being 15$ per half hour and private lessons being 30$ per half hour, we guarantee that you won't find cheaper lessons anywhere else. Founded by Oded Nathan in 2005 (who was fired one year later) Musiotanergy offers all of its 17 years of musical knowledge and expertise to help raise the next generation of musicians in the Wakatipu basin.
